GAME MECHANICS

Introduction

Welcome to Quiz Odyssey, where your journey begins with the creation of questions and progresses through exciting stages. Along the way, you'll encounter challenges, chests, and your trusty chatbot NPC to guide and assist you. Your goal is to navigate through the Houses of Wisdom, Mystery, and Strength by answering questions correctly and using special items like Free Passes and Chatbot help to overcome obstacles.

1. Creating Questions

Before you start playing, you need to create at least 8 questions. Each question must be engaging, and you can set the correct answer and provide multiple-choice options. Once your question set is ready, you can proceed to the game.

Steps to Create Questions:

  • Enter the question creation mode
  • Create at least 9 questions
  • Add answers and options for each question (one correct answer and several distractors)
  • Once you've added 9 or more questions, you can begin playing the game!

2. The Stages of Quiz Odyssey

There are 3 main stages that you must complete in sequence to advance. You will need to complete each stage before progressing to the next:

Stage 1: House of Wisdom

  • Objective: Answer a series of questions to earn wisdom.
  • Rule: You must complete this stage before advancing to Stage 2

Stage 2: House of Mystery

  • Objective: Solve the questions and uncover the mysteries of the house.
  • Rule: You cannot enter this stage until you have completed Stage 1

Stage 3: House of Strength

  • Objective: Answer questions that challenge your knowledge and skills.
  • Rule: You must finish Stage 2 before advancing to Stage 3

3. Treasure Chests

Throughout the game, you'll come across chests that contain various rewards or challenges. There are two types of chests:

Special Chest (Free Pass)

  • Reward: A Free Pass that allows you to skip one question if you're stuck
  • How to Use: Once you get a Free Pass, you can use it during any stage if you're struggling with a question. Simply use the Free Pass option to skip that question and continue your journey

Other Chests (Disappointing Rewards)

Sometimes, you'll open a chest and get a disappointing reward. These chests have humorous or cheeky messages like:

  • "Better luck next time!"
  • "Oops, no luck today!"
  • "Looks like a dud!"
  • "No luck, sorry!"

It's all part of the game, so don't be discouraged! Keep trying and moving forward.

4. Chatbot NPC Assistance

The Chatbot NPC is your loyal companion throughout the game. You can interact with the chatbot at any time to get help or learn more about the game mechanics.

Features of the Chatbot:

Ask the Chatbot for Help:

  • Type 'help' to get a quick overview of the game mechanics and how to proceed
  • Type 'houses' to learn more about the 3 Houses (Wisdom, Mystery, Strength)
  • Type 'chest' to discover more about the treasure chests and what's inside

Free Reveal Questions:

  • The Chatbot can provide up to 3 free reveals for questions
  • Type 'reveal [question number]' to get a hint or a clue for a specific question

Buddy System: Chatbot NPC - Interactive Conversations

The Buddy System is your trusty assistant throughout your journey. The Chatbot behaves like a friendly companion, guiding you through challenges, offering hints, and even cracking a joke when needed. It can help you out when you get stuck and provide casual, human-like interactions.

5. Game Flow and Progression

Starting the Game:

  • Once you've created 9 or more questions, you can begin the game
  • The game starts at Stage 1: House of Wisdom

Progressing Through Stages:

  • Stage 1: Complete the questions to proceed to Stage 2
  • Stage 2: Solve puzzles and riddles to unlock Stage 3
  • Stage 3: Answer final questions to complete the game

Rewards and Achievements:

  • As you complete each stage, you unlock rewards such as more treasure chests, helpful hints from the Chatbot, and more questions
  • Complete all 3 stages to earn the title of Quiz Odyssey Master

6. Game Commands and Interactions

Here are some useful commands to interact with the game:

  • 'help': Get a general overview of the game
  • 'houses': Learn about the different houses types
  • 'chest': Get information on chests and their contents
  • 'reveal [question number]': Ask the chatbot to reveal the answer or provide a hint

7. Pro Tips

  • Use the Free Pass wisely: Don't waste it too early—save it for the more difficult questions!
  • Interact with the Chatbot: It's a helpful guide that can provide valuable hints and insights
  • Complete the stages sequentially: You need to finish each stage to move on to the next
  • Create engaging questions: The more challenging and creative your questions, the more rewarding your quiz journey will be
